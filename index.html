About Pharmacy Support System

The pharmacy support system was built for the sole purpose of assisting the Zambia Medication Reguratory Authority (ZAMRA) in their various tasks the ensure the safe distribution of medication in Zambia.

Services Offered

The services of Pharmacy support system have a crucial role to play in the distribution of medication. The services are as follows:

  • Certifying qualified pharmacists.
  • Providing legal suplliers of pharmaceutical products.
  • Providing an interface for pharmacists to upload thier accounting books for scrutiny.
  • Providing thier inventory recipts.
  • Ensuring every pharmacy abides by the ZAMRA regulations.
